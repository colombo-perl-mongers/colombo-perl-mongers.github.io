Welcome to Colombo Perl Mongers!

 

Perl Mongers is a loose association of international Perl User Groups. Colombo Perl Mongers is a group located in Colombo, Sri Lanka where folks interested in the Perl language can come together to mingle, share ideas and socialize with like-minded individuals in between coding breaks.

How do I join?

After you join, you can interact with fellow Perl Mongers, attend meetups in Colombo and learn more about Perl. Looking forward to seeing you at the next meetup!
